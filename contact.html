Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

How can I track my Apple order?

You can track your order by signing in to your Apple account and visiting the Order Status page. You'll find detailed information on your order status and tracking updates.

What is the return policy?

Apple provides a 14-day return policy for most items purchased from the Apple Store. Make sure the product is in its original condition with all packaging and accessories.

How do I reset my Apple ID password?

You can reset your Apple ID password using the iForgot website. Follow the instructions to securely change your password.

Can I get help with AppleCare+ coverage?

Yes! Contact Apple Support or visit your nearest Apple Store for assistance with your AppleCare+ coverage and services.
